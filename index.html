Methodology

The theme for the project is 'dark romantic'.

It provids an ethereal aesthetic that unifies the black pillar candle as the chosen moving object. A glass crystal was used as a reflective item, along with a yellow apple to give contrast to colour palette. To enhance the visuals and help with the composition, a purple succulent was included as well as a bird skull, and scattered potpourri.

For the lighting, I used an LED ring light with a purple hue as the Key Light to create a dark yet romantic ambience, while a cool lightbulb was used as the Rim Light to add definition to the composition and create a colour contrast. Since I could not adjust the brightness, I placed the rim light at a distance to stop it from emitting a harsh light. The third light was purposely not included to create a dark ambience in line with the theme.

As for the camera settings, it had a 1/20 shutter speed with an F4.5 aperture. Unfortunately, I was unable to capture motion blur. The candle flame did not move fast enough, and using a higher shutter speed lowered the framerate drastically, thus making the video feel choppy.

In the post-editing phase, I incorporated a combination of an ethereal song paired with recordings of fire cracking and chanting to create the dark ambience for the theme. Furthermore, I synced the timing of the lights with the music.
